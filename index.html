About Me

I’m a 29 year old Gold Coast–based engineer with a background in developing industrial control systems — now pivoting into the world of front-end web development. Originally from the UK, I bring over 10 years of experience working as a SCADA Engineer across high-stakes industries like rail, airports, and water infrastructure, both nationally and internationally. My journey began with a Siemens apprenticeship, followed by a BSc (Hons) in Electrical and Electronic Engineering. I'm a proud member of the IET with Engineering Technician accreditation and currently on the path to achieving Incorporated Engineer (IEng) status. In 2024, I relocated to Australia and began a new chapter — combining my systems-thinking mindset and passion for interface design to build modern, responsive web applications. My goal is to create intuitive, user-focused digital experiences while continuously growing as a front-end developer. When I’m not coding, you’ll find me exploring the Gold Coast with my partner and 3 year cavoodle dog, eating yummy brunches and taking long walks along the beach.
